Unit 2 : Fun with image, Optimization and formal analysis

Fun with image : Image tranformation was totally new for me. Studying about pixels and how tiny information are stored in an image can be compared with the concept of atoms and molecules (if we zoom into the concept). I personally liked the idea of connecting Blurring an image with matrices. Most of the transformation of images topics can be related with studying algebra and geometry, for example reflection of an image was like reflecting a straight line in its opposite quardrant.

Optimization and formal analysis: what I learned is finding all the possible solution and then taking the best possible solution, makes us find the most effective solution. Greedy vs Dynamic Programming: useful, but i feel like writting and making graph everytime is time taking, so we can use greedy approach sometimes.

Train graph: It was simple yet effective solution. I feel that train-time graphs are a good solution for real time .

Bridges of konigsberg: It was an interesting problem. In its solution I want to add something - if we remove any one bridge minimum, we can cover each bridge.

For covering every bridge exactly once, we have to make at least a closed figure, in one of the ways I can think of it as two sides attached to one closed figure, no matter how longer sides are but at least one closed figure is required to walk across the path and reach at same starting and ending point ( at least two nodes should be of even degrees).
