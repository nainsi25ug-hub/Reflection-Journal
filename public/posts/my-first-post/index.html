I am Nainsi Priya, a student of Azim Premji University majoring in Information Science disciplinary. I am creating this reflection journal as part of my coursework. I will be jounaling my reflection and learnings of the classes throughout the course.

Unit 1: What is information ?

DIKW Model, Markdown and Wiki, Working with Census Education Data,

In the beginning, we studied about Dikw (data, information, knowledge and wisdome) model. I always wondered whether there exist a method of organizing data in a structured formate and this model gave me answer to that . I can connect it as a hierchial organization order with levels. Also, while working with the activity I noticed that the app ‘Chatgpt’ works as a loop at the knowledge level. The more information it gets, the better and more it will generate output, which made me question the efficiency of the hierchial structure of Dikw then, my instructor explained the doubt in a very effictive and structured manner, which was impressive, as she explains that the problem with this hierchial structure is that there is no defined term or order of progression, which can make it quite confusing to rearrange the data and required information. The question was inside in my head for long and I wondered if there could be some other way of arranging data, I tried to find one so I define this model as " Loop way of working with data", adding to the DIKW model, this model works in accordance with it but the order is not defined and a person working with data can form chains, it works like the chain in the bicycle, where there are no levels and the person can start his/her work choosing any on the time and building the loops to it, adding each segment of the chain according to the necessity. However, this model may take time but gives the person flexibility to work without hierchial order.

By using census education data of bangalore, I learned operating with real time structured data in google sheet. It was new to me. Also, I learned that large amount of data can be opared easily if managed in a structured way.

Dotted graph is not good if we have to plot a large amount of data.

Humans have limitations, the perceptual limits, when dealing with data we should keep it in our mind.
