Bridges of konigsberg: It was an interesting problem. In its solution I  want to add something  - if we remove any one  bridge minimum, we can cover each bridge. 

For covering every bridge exactly once, we have to make at least a closed figure, in one of the ways I can think of it as two sides attached to one closed figure, no matter how longer sides are but at least one closed figure is required to walk across the path and reach at same starting and ending point  ( at least two nodes should be of even degrees).
